1. Who to Target

Best Fit
  • ✅ Private tennis / racquet clubs
  • ✅ Country clubs with tennis program
  • ✅ Athletic clubs with 4+ courts
  • ✅ 30 – 200 active adult members
  • ✅ Already run a ladder manually (Excel, WhatsApp, paper)
  • ✅ Have a Director of Tennis or Tennis Captain
Decision Makers
  • 🎯 Director of Tennis — primary contact
  • 🎯 Head Pro / Tennis Captain — influencer
  • 🎯 Club Manager / GM — budget approval
  • 🎯 Board Member (Sports Dir) — member clubs
Green Flags
  • ⭐ Club website has a "Ladder" or "Rankings" page
  • ⭐ They post match results on social media
  • ⭐ Active adult league / interclub program
  • ⭐ New tennis director (hired in last 12 months)
  • ⭐ Mentions "challenge" system or "round robin"

How to find them fast

"tennis ladder" "private club" [city] site:clubwebsite.com
inurl:staff OR inurl:team "director of tennis" racquetclub

Search clubs that use these platforms — they're already digitally inclined and likely have active ladder programs to upgrade.

8. Objections & How to Handle Them

"We already use [Global Tennis Network / TennisRungs / spreadsheet]"

→ "That's great — most of our best customers came from those. The main difference is members don't need to create an account on a separate platform. They just add the app to their home screen. Would you like to see a side-by-side?"

"It's too expensive / budget is tight"

→ "Totally fair. The Starter plan at $99/season works out to about $1.65/week for the whole club. A lot of directors just add it as a small line item to the annual tennis program budget. Would that structure work?"

"Our members are older / not tech-savvy"

→ "We hear that a lot — and it's usually the directors who are most surprised. If they can use WhatsApp or check email on a phone, they can use Challengers. The entire flow is 3 taps. Want me to show you the onboarding for members?"

"I'd need to run it by the board / general manager"

→ "Of course — here's a one-paragraph summary you can paste into an email to them: [send them a pre-written blurb]. What's typically the decision timeline for something like this at your club?"

"Can we try it for free first?"

→ "Yes — the Starter plan is $99 for the full season. If after 30 days it's not working for your club, I'll refund it. That's the closest thing to a free trial I can offer at this price point." (Only offer if needed to close.)
