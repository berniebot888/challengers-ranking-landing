🎥 How to record the animated reels (1 through 7)

  1. Open the reel link in Chrome (e.g. reel-3-challenge.html).
  2. Resize the browser window to roughly portrait dimensions — about 540 wide × 960 tall. The animation auto-scales to fit. Don't worry about exact pixels — IG re-encodes on upload.
  3. Press Cmd + Shift + 5 to open macOS recording controls.
  4. Click "Record Selected Portion" → drag the selection box over the animation stage (the colored area, not the helper panel in the top-right).
  5. Click "▶ Start animation" on the helper panel (it auto-hides during recording), then click Record in the macOS controls.
  6. Wait for the full animation (8-15s depending on the reel) → stop recording (red bar in menu bar).
  7. The MOV file lands on your desktop. Open in QuickTime, trim if needed, drag into Instagram's Reels editor.

Posting cadence suggestion: 1 reel every 2-3 days. Order matters — start with Reel 1 (social proof "10 days · 68 players"), then Reel 5 (install — useful immediately), then Reels 3, 4, 6, 7 (features). Reel 2 is redundant with Reel 4 — pick one or save Reel 2 for "throwback".
