El ranking de escalera
Esto es un ranking de escalera. Cada socio tiene una posición. Cuanto más alto, más fuerte. La diferencia con un torneo: no es un evento puntual, es un sistema vivo. Las posiciones cambian todo el tiempo, partido a partido. ¿Ves esa espadita al lado del nombre? Significa que ese jugador tiene un desafío activo: alguien lo está retando, o él retó a alguien. Es el pulso del club.
This is a ladder ranking. Every member has a position. Higher means stronger. The difference with a tournament: this isn't an event, it's a living system. Positions change all the time, match by match. See that crossed swords icon? It means that player has an active challenge: someone's playing them, or they're challenging someone else. It's the pulse of the club.

But there's an exception: the wildcard. Every new player gets one as a welcome gift, and earns another by winning three matches in a row. The wildcard lets you challenge any position, no limit. It's the shortcut to climb fast when you're on a streak. Players on a streak become dangerous.

The challenged player has 48 hours to respond. They can accept, or decline with a reason. No reply also counts as a decline. But careful: you can't decline more than two challenges per year, or you lose a position in the ranking as a penalty. There's nowhere to hide.
Tono: con onda. "Acá no se puede esconder" → como sentencia, firme.

Escena 8 / 14
Cargar resultado
Cualquiera de los dos jugadores carga el score, y el ranking se actualiza al instante. Confiamos en que carguen el resultado correcto. Si hubo una equivocación, se puede corregir. Si el desafiante ganó, sube justo arriba del que perdió. Y si era un desafío con wild card, esa wild card ya se consumió.
Either player enters the score, and the ranking updates instantly. We trust them to log the right result. If there was a mistake, it can be corrected. If the challenger won, they move up, right above the player they beat. And if it was a wildcard challenge, the wildcard is now used up.
Tono: confiable, casual. "Confiamos" como punto fuerte.
