When you send a challenge, your opponent has 2 days to respond. If they don't reply in those 48 hours, it counts as a rejection and you can go challenge someone else. If they accept, you both have 10 days to play the match. How do you coordinate day, time, and court? Each challenge has its own chat built into the app, no need to jump to WhatsApp or set up a group. And one important detail: the challenger brings the balls.
