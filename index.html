Common questions

FAQ

Do players need to download anything?

No. Challengers Ranking is a Progressive Web App. Players open a link in their browser and add it to their home screen in one tap — it looks and works just like a native app.

How long does setup take?

We handle the setup for you. Send us your player list and we have your club's ranking live within 24 hours. After that, players register themselves.

Can we run separate rankings for men and women?

Yes. Plans include multiple rankings (Men's Singles, Women's Singles, Mixed Doubles, etc.). Pricing is per club: Starter (2 rankings, 50 players, $49/mo), Growth (4 rankings, 150 players, $99/mo), or Pro (unlimited rankings, 300 players, $179/mo).

What happens when a player leaves the club?

The admin can remove or deactivate any player instantly. The rest of the ranking adjusts automatically.

Does Challengers Ranking work for padel clubs?

Yes — we're built as a padel ladder app for clubs. The padel ranking supports singles, doubles and mixed doubles, with a full pair-management system (invitations, history, stats per pair) tailored for the way padel is actually played. Live standings, automated challenges, deadlines, match reporting and a community feed all included. Same plan, same admin — no extra fee for adding the padel ranking.

Can I run a pickleball ladder ranking for my club?

Yes. The pickleball ladder uses the standard game format (game-to-11, win by 2). Run a pickleball ranking alongside or instead of tennis — same admin, same plan, same player accounts. Singles, doubles, automated challenges, member chat and stats included. Players install it as a PWA — no App Store download needed.

Is there a squash ladder app option?

Yes. We work as a squash ladder app for clubs running internal competitive ladders. Squash ranking supports the standard scoring (best of 3 or 5 games to 11) and the same automation as tennis and padel: 48-hour challenge response, deadline-based expirations, walk-overs and live ranking updates. Replaces a spreadsheet or whiteboard squash ladder in 24 hours.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime — your data is always yours to export.
