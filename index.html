Common questions

FAQ

Do players need to download anything?

No. Challengers Ranking is a Progressive Web App. Players open a link in their browser and add it to their home screen in one tap — it looks and works just like a native app.

How long does setup take?

We handle the setup for you. Send us your player list and we have your club's ranking live within 24 hours. After that, players register themselves.

Can we run separate rankings for men and women?

Yes. Every plan supports multiple independent rankings — Men's Singles, Women's Singles, Mixed Doubles, or any category you need. Each ranking is billed separately at $99/month for up to 100 players.

What happens when a player leaves the club?

The admin can remove or deactivate any player instantly. The rest of the ranking adjusts automatically.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime — your data is always yours to export.
